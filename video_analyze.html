Is this an editing quality score?

No. The scanner focuses on business consequences: clarity, trust, attention, product understanding and CTA friction.

What do I receive in the free report?

You receive the overall readiness score, buyer loss risk and the first three conversion leaks detected. Additional opportunities remain available in the full blueprint.

Can I scan a YouTube video?

Yes. The first version is designed around a YouTube URL.

Does it replace a marketing strategist?

No. It gives your team a structured first-pass audit so strategic attention goes to the highest-impact issues first.
